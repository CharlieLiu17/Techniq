Where perfection meets reality.

Techniq is a state of the art pose tracking program that helps you become your best. Input two images, one of your form and one of the professional athlete you would like to compare yourself to. Using MediaPipe and OpenCV pose detection technology, Techniq creates a graph of coordinate points that map the position of your body relative to the position of another body. Using the differences in the coordinates, Techniq will tell you the approximate degree you need to adjust your body to achieve perfect form.
